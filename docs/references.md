# References

## Purpose

This document lists authoritative references relevant to the mathematical
and cryptographic foundations of Shamir Secret Sharing (SSS).

The references provided here support the assumptions and validation approach
used by the `shamir-sss-validation` repository.

---

## Primary Reference

- A. Shamir, "How to Share a Secret", Communications of the ACM, Vol. 22, No. 11, 1979

## Cryptographic Literature
